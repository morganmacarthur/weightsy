# Privacy Promise

weightsy is designed to collect the minimum data necessary to provide core tracking functionality.

## We aim to do

- Minimize identifying data.
- Be explicit about stored fields.
- Avoid ad-tech style tracking.
- Keep the architecture understandable by non-developers.

## We do not aim to do

- Build ad profiles.
- Sell personal data.
- Collect unnecessary personal attributes.

## Communication standard

Any change to stored data fields should be reflected in `DATA_SCHEMA.md`.
